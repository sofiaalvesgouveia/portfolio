Sofia Gouveia

About

Born and raised in Porto, Sofia Gouveia is a portuguese communication designer. She first discovered her love for arts and design back in secondary school at Escola Artística Soares dos Reis, and later refined her Communication Design skills at Faculdade de Belas Artes da Universidade do Porto.

Her main areas of interest include Editorial Design, Photography, Branding, and Web Design. Outside of design, she finds inspiration (and distraction) in many places: knitting and crochet, sudoku puzzles and books that she always starts but almost never finishes.

When she’s not experimenting with grids, typefaces, or color palettes, you’ll most likely find her happily distracted by cats (and yes, she firmly believes every great designer should have at least one feline muse). If creativity runs on coffee, hers runs on purring companionship and the occasional cat hair on the keyboard.
