About

As part of the Product Design course within my Communication Design degree, I developed a packaging project for safely transporting and protecting a plant of choice. I selected the cactus Cephalocereus senilis.

The packaging was designed to avoid direct contact with the plant’s spines while ensuring ventilation. After several prototypes, I created a modular structure composed of four interlocking pieces that require no glue. These elements adapt to different vase and plant heights, providing flexibility and stability.
